Visit Chesterfield

Enjoy nature and history in this beautiful county located in Central Virginia

Top three activities to do in Chesterfield:

Visit Pocohontas State Park for hiking, watersports, and other outdoor activities.

The Chesterfield Government Center is home to several historical buildings open to the public.

Rockwood Park and Daniel Park are both excellent locations for sports and fitness activities including Pickleball, Tennis, Soccer, Football, and Basketball.
